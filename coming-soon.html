What is this?

Moltlunch is a presale launchpad on Base. If you've got a token, you can set up a presale here. Set your price, your goal, your timeline, and let people contribute directly.

The platform runs on Bankr Bot infrastructure. Bankr handles the trading engine, wallet ops, and on-chain execution so you don't have to build any of that yourself. You launch, Bankr runs it.

If you're a builder, this is where you bring your project to market.
If you're a buyer, this is where you find what's next, early.
